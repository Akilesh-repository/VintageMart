Who We Are

Curators of the
Extraordinary.

VintageMart began as a humble storefront in the antiques district of Charleston, South Carolina. Over the past three decades, we have evolved into a globally recognized purveyor of fine vintage and antique furnishings. We believe that true luxury lies in the history and craftsmanship of the past.

Unlike conventional retailers, our organization acts as an archival gallery. Every artifact that passes through our doors is treated with the utmost respect. We employ a dedicated team of restorers, appraisers, and historians who ensure that the legacy of each piece is documented, preserved, and prepared for its next lifetime of use.

Our Core Purpose

The guiding principles that dictate every acquisition, restoration, and client interaction at VintageMart.

The Mission

Ethical Preservation

To rescue, restore, and ethically preserve historical furniture and decor. We are committed to preventing the loss of masterful craftsmanship by identifying pieces of significance and reviving them using period-accurate techniques and sustainable restoration practices.

  • Zero-compromise authentic restoration.
  • Sustainable sourcing and material recycling.
The Vision

A Legacy in Every Home

We envision a world where modern spaces are grounded by the weight of history. We strive to be the premier global destination for collectors and designers seeking to integrate the unmatched elegance and stories of antique furnishings into contemporary environments.

  • Fostering appreciation for historical design.
  • Building lifelong relationships with collectors.
Our Journey

The Timeline

1994

The First Gallery

VintageMart opens its doors in a small 800 sq ft location in Charleston, SC, focusing exclusively on local estate liquidations and Victorian-era parlor pieces.

2002

Global Sourcing Begins

The team expands operations to Europe, establishing relationships with specialized pickers in France and Italy, bringing authentic Mid-Century Modern and Art Deco pieces stateside.

2015

The Restoration Atelier

Opening of our dedicated 10,000 sq ft restoration facility, allowing us to control the entire preservation process in-house using historically accurate materials and techniques.

Today

Digital & Private Registry

Launch of our private client network and digital provenance archives, allowing collectors worldwide to securely verify and acquire museum-quality antiquities from the comfort of their homes.

The Acquisition Process

How an artifact goes from a forgotten estate to our curated gallery floor. A rigorous standard of quality control.

1

Discovery & Sourcing

Our scouts attend exclusive estate sales, private auctions, and collaborate with European pickers to uncover pieces with exceptional potential and documented lineage.

2

Verification & Appraisal

Master appraisers cross-reference maker's marks, materials, and historical records to certify the era and authenticity of the artifact.

3

Sympathetic Restoration

The piece is moved to our atelier. We stabilize and clean the item, utilizing era-appropriate finishes to restore its glory without erasing its historical patina.
