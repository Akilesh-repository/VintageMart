Atelier Restoration Rates

Our restoration atelier operates on a strict tier system based on the level of intervention required. All work utilizes historically accurate methods.

Tier I

Sympathetic Touch-Up

Starting at $450 per piece

Designed for pieces in excellent structural condition that require minor aesthetic revitalization. We focus on preserving the original patina while stabilizing the finish.

  • Gentle surface cleaning
  • Hand-rubbed wax finish
  • Hardware polishing
  • Minor veneer fills
Request Tier I Assessment
Tier II

Comprehensive Revival

Starting at $1,200 per piece

Required when a piece has suffered environmental damage, structural loosening, or previous improper restorations. This involves taking the piece down to its bare elements.

  • Full finish stripping
  • Joint re-gluing & doweling
  • Custom French polishing
  • Upholstery teardown
Request Tier II Assessment
Tier III

Museum-Grade Rebuild

Consultation Required

Reserved for highly significant artifacts from the 18th century or earlier. We utilize only era-specific, historically documented adhesives, dyes, and organic fibers to recreate lost components.

  • Hide glue applications
  • Hand-carved replacement parts
  • Horsehair & coil spring seating
  • Full photographic documentation
Inquire with the Atelier
